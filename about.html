About Study Planner

Plan smart, track progress, and stay motivated.

Smart Cozy Focused Timetables

What this app does

Study Planner helps you create daily plans, manage exam timetables, check off tasks, and see your progress clearly. It keeps everything simple, friendly, and focused on your goals.

Key Features

📝
Daily Plans

Create subject-topic lists for each day and keep them organized.

🗓️
Exam Timetables

Create, edit, and organize exam schedules with subjects, dates, and time slots.

📈
Progress Tracking

View a clean progress bar for Today, Weekly, or Monthly progress.

✨
Rewards

Complete all tasks in a day to earn coins and unlock levels.

✏️
Edit Anytime

Update your plan, add items, or delete a day when needed.
